Dino World Tutorial

Hello and welcome to the dino world tutorial. Here we are goign to explain everything about our app along with a video you can follow. First you will end up on the landing page with and explore button, clck it to move to the next screen. You are now on the periods page, Dinos change depending on the period you choose so we are going to choose cretaceous.
Now that we have chosen our period it is time to choose a continent, for learning purposes we are going to choose Africa. In Africa there are many different dinos, let's choose the cool green one in the corner. This is the dino page right now you can only see the egg but if you click on it your new dino buddy will appear. There are three buttons located near the bottom. the "i" is for dino information the spoon and fork are food options to feed your dinosaur and the back arrow is the reset button to return your dino back to egg form
from here just click on the food icons and begin to feed your dino, you will notice it growing in size until.... tada you r new adult dino buddy is created. feel free to hit the back arrow in the top left of the screen to go back to the continents page.
