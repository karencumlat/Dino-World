About The App
Hello, and welcome to our app Dino world! Dino world is an app build to teach young kids about dinosaurs in a fun and interactive environemt. The app covers a range of dinosaurs from the fericous carnivores like the T-rex from the Cretaceous period to the massive herbivores like the Stegosaurus in the jurrasic period. Our goal is to invoke a cool and exciting environment making it easy for kids to revisit and learn more. Enjoy!
